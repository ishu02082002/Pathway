    </div>
    <div class="div-content-elements-text">
      <p>
        Actuarial science is now in India and slowly it is getting popularity.
        Actuarial science basically involves knowledge of mathematics,
        statistics, insurance, and investment. B.sc Actuarial Science is a

        demand for Actuaries outside India.
